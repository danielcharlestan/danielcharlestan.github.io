Summer 2014 Research Assistantship

I worked with Associate Dean of Research Fernando Lozano as a research assistant in Summer 2014. I worked exclusively on his project with the Institute for the Study of Labor . This publication, tiled Rank-Order Tournaments, Probability of Winning and Investing in Talent: Evidence from Champions League Qualifying Rules

This paper analyzes how a change in the probability of winning a tournament affects an effort. In particular, we observed a soccer team’s behavior using the qualification rules for entry into the group and playoff stages of the UEFA Champions' League. Our results suggest that increasing the number of slots that a national league gets in the Champions' League leads to increases in investment in talent ex-ante.
